Cake Pricing

Basic Cakes

Our basic cake prices are based on a single-flavor three layer cake and include a basic buttercream frosting, drizzle, and some kind of simple topping. All of our basic cakes can be customized to fit your theme, but customizations will affect pricing. The prices listed here are primarily to serve as a ballpark figure for estimating costs.

We are always looking to expand our flavor options. If you want something that you don't see listed here, please let us know and we will do our best to accommodate any requests!

Cake Flavors

  • Chocolate
  • Vanilla

Frosting Flavors

  • Chocolate Buttercream
  • Vanilla Buttercream

Prices

  • 3 Layer 6" Round: $50
  • 3 Layer 8" Round: $65
  • 3 Layer 10" Round: $80

Deluxe Cakes

Customizations or add-ons to the basic cakes are considered Deluxe Cakes and carry an increased cost. Shown below are some examples of cakes that are considered Deluxe Cakes. Each of the cakes follows the Delux Cake Prices list. We are happy to make further customizations but keep in mind that all modifications may affect pricing.

Chocolate Reeses Cake

Chocolate cake with peanut butter buttercream. Chopped Reeses and chocolate ganache in between each layer. Topped with Reeses pieces and mini peanut butter cups.

Chocolate Chip Brownie Cake

Brownie cake with chocolate chip cookie dough frosting and chocolate ganache drizzle.

Caramel Cappuccino Cake

Cappuccino cake with coffee-soak and caramel buttercream.

Funfetti Cake

Funfetti Cake with funfetti frosting and pink ganache drizzle.

Deluxe Cake Prices

  • 3 Layer 6" Round: $65
  • 3 Layer 8" Round: $80
  • 3 Layer 10" Round: $95
