Cupcake Pricing

Classic Cupcakes

Our classic cupcake prices are based on dozen cupcakes with a simple buttercream frosting and simple topping like sprinkles or luster-dust. Any flavor or color combinations other than the classic chocolate or vanilla will be $22/dozen.
Fillings (milk/semi-sweet/white chocolate, caramel, etc) will be an additional $5/dozen.
Extra toppings* (candies, toppers, drizzles, etc.) will be an additional $5/dozen.

We are always looking to expand our flavor options. If you want something that you don't see listed here, please let us know and we will do our best to accommodate any requests!

Cupcake Flavors

  • Chocolate
  • Vanilla

Frosting Flavors

  • Chocolate Buttercream
  • Vanilla Buttercream

Prices

  • Classic Chocolate/Vanilla: $20/dozen
  • Custom Flavors/Colors: $22/dozen
  • Fillings: +$5/dozen
  • Extra Toppings*: +$5/dozen
*Time consuming topping requests (see unicorn cupcakes below) will incur an additional charge.

These cupcakes would cost $22/dozen due to the custom flavored cake/frosting.
