About

The Butter Nut Bakery is a small home baking operation located in Albany, Oregon. We specialize in custom cakes and cupcakes made to order. We use high-quality ingredients and always try to go the extra mile to put a smile on our customers' faces. Please take a minute to check out our portfolio or read our story. Have a question?
